How to Install ROR VANSALE APP on Your Tab

Follow these simple steps. No technical knowledge needed.

1

Step 1: Get the app file

Tap the green button below. This will start downloading the app to your tablet.

CLICK THE DOWNLOAD BUTTON

After you tap, go to Step 2 below.

2

Step 2: When you see “Download file again?”

Your tablet may show a small box asking to download again. Do this:

Tap the blue “Download again” button.

This saves the app file (app-release.apk) on your device.

3

Step 3: Open your Downloads

In your browser (Chrome or the app you used to download), do this:

Then a menu will open. Tap "Downloads" in that menu:

  • Tap the three dots (⋮) at the top right of the browser.
  • In the menu that opens, tap “Downloads”.

You will see a list of your downloaded files.

4

Step 4: Install or update the app

In the Downloads list, find the file named app-release.apk (or app-release (1).apk).

Tap on app-release.apk. Then tap Install or Update when your tablet asks.

Done! The ROR VANSALE app will open after installation.

Thank you. If you need help, ask someone who has used the app before.
